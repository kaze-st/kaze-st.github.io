Welcome!

Welcome to my personal website!

My name is Hao, and I am majoring in computer science at the University of Washington. Computer science allows me to channel my creativity towards advancing society and improving people’s lives. I'm most passionate about the applications of machine learning in medicine. I lost a close friend to leukemia, and I'm extremely motivated to make a difference in the lives of others battling diseases of their own. I believe I can best serve the medical field as a computer scientist. In the meantime, software development allows me to creatively design and logically implement apps targeting people in need. One of the projects that I have collaborated on is called WeFund, an app that manages group donations to crowdsource basic needs among those who can't afford it on their own.

I was born and raised in Spain. Outside of computer science, I like to play guitar and improvise a 12-bar blues with backing tracks. Coming to Seattle made me miss the culinary culture of my home country. However, I turned my homesickness into an opportunity to employ my love for cooking, and I regularly cook traditional dishes from my home country.
